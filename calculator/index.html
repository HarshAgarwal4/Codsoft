General Instructions for using this calculator

  • '+' ---- > used to add two numbers
  • '-' ---- > used to subtract two numbers
  • '*' ---- > used to multiply two numbers
  • '/' ---- > used to division two numbers
  • '**' ---- > power operator eg:- 2**6 = 64 means 2 to the power 6
  • 'c' ---- > clears screen
  • 'DEl' ---- > DEL number or Backspace
  • '%' ---- > used to get remainder eg:- if you write 5%2
    you will get 1 not 5% of 2 due to fact 5/2 leaves remainder 1
